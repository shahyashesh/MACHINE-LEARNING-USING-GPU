Why Use GPU for Machine Learning?

GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) is a game-changer in the field of machine learning. With parallel processing capabilities, it significantly speeds up training times and allows you to work with larger and more complex datasets. It's the key to tackling deep learning and complex neural network models effectively.
